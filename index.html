Animal Monitoring System

Tracking Animals

The Animal Monitoring System is a prototype device designed to track the location and monitor the health of animals, measuring parameters such as body temperature and humidity.

Cost-Efficient

This system offers a cost-effective solution to help small-scale farmers avoid expensive commercial monitoring systems.
